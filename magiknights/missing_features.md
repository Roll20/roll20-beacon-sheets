# Missing Character Sheet Features

Rules from the Magi-Knights compendium that should appear on the character sheet but are not yet implemented.

---

## 1. Attrition Mechanics

- **Endurance Die toggle/indicator**: Whether the d6 roll applies to Stress/Exhaustion checks
- **Freaking Out tracker**: Daily "Oppressive Stress" flag
- **Heartless Knight status** (auto at 3+ Corruption): -1 SP gained, no Catharsis participation
- **Fallen Knight status** (auto at 5+ Corruption): Half Trauma received, Risk of Relapse flag
- **Burnout Lines counter**: Permanent lines on Soul Eclipse (currently tracked visually but not enforced)
- **Acts of Remorse tracker**: Progress toward Redemption for each Corruption point

## 2. Character Creation Options (Mechanical Effects)

- **Student Type abilities**: 8 types with unique bonuses (field exists, mechanics not applied)
- **Court Card of Fate effects**: King, Queen, Knight, Dame, Squire, Damsel mechanical bonuses
- **Player Link bonuses**: 13+ link options affecting stats/abilities
- **Magic Style restrictions**: Which spell paths are available based on style

## 3. Magi-Squire Companion

- Health Blips (6)
- Mana Blips (3)
- Armor values (13 Student / 15 Knight)
- Damage scaling (1d6+3 to 4d6)
- Spell path access (Beam, Explosion, Curing, Restoration only)
- Level tracking (synced to mentor)
- Squire Skills list

## 4. Combat Forms

- Current Combat Form selection (I-X)
- Form Mastery checkboxes/unlocks
- Form-specific bonuses applied to rolls

## 5. Level-Locked Ability Tracking

- Counter Blast (unlocks at 5th)
- Perfect Parry (unlocks at 6th)
- Extricate Aether (unlocks at 6th)
- Heroic Resolve (unlocks at 9th)
- Knight's Insight (unlocks at 9th)
- Knight's Resolution (unlocks at 9th)

## 6. Spell Path Mechanics

- Spell path access by Magic Style (which paths character can learn)
- Spell Modification options with Exhaustion cost tracking

## 7. Relic System

- Relic capacity display (max = Reputation Level)
- Relic slot enforcement

## 8. Social System Tracking

- **Heart Stage** per NPC/Bond (Threatening, Hostile, Cold, Neutral, Warm, Friendly, Sympathetic)
- **HIRU flags** per NPC (recognized/not recognized)

## 9. Missing Conditions in Tracker

- **Disoriented** (Disadvantage on Attacks, Physical Resists, Skill Checks)
- **Unconscious** (full tracking with Prone + Student Persona revert)

## 10. Rolls to Resist Modifiers

- Per-type Advantage/Disadvantage toggles (Physical, Magic, Horror, Purity)
- Resist proficiency tracking per type

## 11. Skill Mastery

- Mastery unlock indicators per skill at certain levels

## 12. Sleep Phase Effects

- Current sleep effect selector: Average / Feverish / Refreshing (affects recovery)

## 13. Daily/Session Limits

- Crystalline Seal Implantation (1/day given, 1/day received)
- Medical Triage usage per target (1/Sleep Phase)
- Soul Sacrifice count (Reputation Level times per career)
